Project: Website Design for Vital Construction
We redesigned Vital Construction’s website to enhance their online presence and showcase their construction expertise. The new site features a modern, responsive design with intuitive navigation, a detailed services section, and an impressive project portfolio. Integrated with a user-friendly CMS and optimized for SEO, the website now effectively attracts and engages potential clients, highlighting Vital Construction’s commitment to quality and excellence.
Project: Website Design for Jessica Chan Naturopathic Medicine

We revamped Jessica Chan Naturopathic Medicine’s website to better reflect her holistic approach to healthcare. The redesigned site features a calming, user-friendly design with sections for services, patient testimonials, and wellness resources. Fully responsive and SEO-optimized, the website enhances user engagement and makes it easy for patients to book appointments and access information.
